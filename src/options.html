Keyboard shortcuts

Click a binding, then press the new key combination. While recording: Esc cancels, Backspace clears the slot. Each action can have two bindings.

Always fixed: Enter / Shift+Enter navigate matches, Esc always closes, adding Shift to "open link in new tab" switches to the new tab. The browser-wide toggle (Ctrl+Shift+F) is managed by Chrome at chrome://extensions/shortcuts.
